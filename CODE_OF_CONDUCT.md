# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as members, contributors, and leaders, pledge to make participation in the Aegis community a harassment-free and professional experience for everyone. We value technical excellence, respectful debate, and the diverse perspectives of our global community.

## Professional Standards
- **Empathy & Respect**: Demonstrate kindness and professional courtesy toward others.
- **Constructive Feedback**: Give and gracefully accept high-signal, technical feedback.
- **Accountability**: Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.
- **Community Focus**: Prioritize the long-term health and integrity of the project.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. We reserve the right to remove or reject contributions, comments, or other behaviors that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
