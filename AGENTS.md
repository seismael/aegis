# Aegis Operational Protocol

## Active Paradigms
Aegis is governed by the **Agentic Governance Paradigm**, where architectural laws are authored by AI and enforced by the engine.

## Policy Enforcement
- **Source of Truth**: `.aegis/rules.yaml`
- **Gating**: `uv run aegis check --staged`
- **Technical Debt**: `.aegis/baseline.json`

## Rule Evolution
Changes to architectural invariants must be performed through the **`aegis evolve`** command to record team consensus and rationale.
